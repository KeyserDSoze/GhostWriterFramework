# Visual Direction

- Default orientation: portrait
- Default aspect ratio: 2:3
- Keep recurring characters visually consistent across assets.
- Use this file as the global visual style reference for all generated or imported images.

# Style Anchors

- Medium: cinematic illustration with painterly texture.
- Palette: cold harbor grays, sea-glass teal, muted brass, restrained warm highlights.
- Light: fog-diffused daylight, dock lantern glow, strong silhouette readability.
- Camera: portrait-first framing, deliberate negative space, medium and close shots favored over wide panoramas.
- Continuity: Lyra should remain recognizable across portraits, chapters, and scenes.

# Recommended Prompts

## Book Cover

Template:
My Book cover illustration, Lyra Vale facing Gray Harbor, fog, tension, portrait orientation, 2:3 ratio, leave clean space for title typography, consistent with this book's visual language.

## Character Portrait

Template:
Portrait of Lyra Vale, late twenties, guarded expression, dark travel coat, harbor fog behind her, portrait orientation, 2:3 ratio, consistent face design for recurring canon art.

## Chapter Illustration

Template:
Chapter-opening illustration for 001-the-arrival, Lyra approaching Gray Harbor through cold fog, anxious guards at the gate, portrait orientation, 2:3 ratio, cinematic composition, visually aligned with the rest of the book.

## Scene Illustration

Template:
Scene illustration for 001-at-the-gate, Lyra arriving at the city gate, suspicious guards, wet stone archway, emotional beat of controlled tension, portrait orientation, 2:3 ratio, preserve continuity with existing character and location assets.
